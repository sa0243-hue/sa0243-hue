Jujutsu kaisen

In a world where curses are born from negative human emotions, there are people known as Jujutsu Sorcerers. These sorcerers protect humans from dangerous curses. Yuji Itadori ( the main character) is an ordinary high school student until he eats one of the fingers of the ancient King of Curses, Ryomen Sukuna, and becomes his vessel. This thrusts him into the dark world of curses and monsters. Yuji joins Jujutsu High, a school for Jujutsu Sorcerers, and leaves his old life behind. Along the way, he meets his classmates Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki. This hit anime series currently has 3 seasons out right now in 2026 june and is said to be around 4-5 seasons with the fourth in making. This anime series went viral in 2020 after it aired as an anime.

Yuji's parents died when he was young and he lived with his grandpa who was very sick and died in the first episode. Yuji and his 2 friends found the curse object (the finger) and his friends decided to take a look after school. Meanwhile Megumi Fushiguro, a first year at jujutsu high, has come to check on the curse object but finds it missing. So he looks for it to sense traces of it on Yuji's backpack, he follows Yuji to the hospital where Yuji's grandpa passed away. He then realized the cursed object isn't with him and explains the dangers of it and how it can attract other curses who want to eat the finger to gain immense power. Yuji says his friends planned on opening it at the school so they rush there. They go to find Yuji's friends already getting attacked by curses and they save them. But then a big one comes out of nowhere and attacks them. Yuji realizes they're all going to die if he doesn't do something so he has the bright idea of eating the cursed object. Recalling what Megumi said earlier about gaining power, he just wanted to be strong enough to save them. Well, that was not a smart idea because it has a 99% chance of killing him. But a rare thing occurred where he became Ryomen Sukuna's vessel and Sukuna is released on the world.
