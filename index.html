Jujutsu kaisen

In a world where curses are born from negative human emotions, there are people known as Jujutsu Sorcerers. These sorcerers protect humans from dangerous curses. Yuji Itadori( the main character) is an ordinary high school student until he eats one of the fingers of the ancient King of Curses, Ryomen Sukuna, and becomes his vessel. This thrusts him into the dark world of curses and monsters. Yuji joins Jujutsu High, a school for Jujutsu Sorcerers, and leaves his old life behind. Along the way, he meets his classmates Megumi Fushiguro and Nobara Kugisaki.
